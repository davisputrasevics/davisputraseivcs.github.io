The challenge.

We as leaders have a tsunami of information coming our way from different sources. And even with great memory and note taking skills, it is often difficult to follow along and remember it all. And when/if we take notes of everything, it is easy to get lost in all thoes paragraphs of information.

The purpose.

The purpose of this application is to help team leaders in one area of that challenge - sorting and standartizing information from 1:1 conversations with their employees, and make the most important of accessable to the manager with few clicks of a button and without tedious searching.

How to use the application.

The system recognizes specific keywords and symbols from your notes and extracts the most important information to make it easily visable to you. The text between the starting keyword and the ending symbol will be extracted. As of now the system recognizes following keywords:

  • *idea
  • *team
  • *challenge

    • Each of these keywords has to begin with an asterisk (*) which is directly followed by the keyword, and each thought in the text also has to end with an asterisk without any simbols directly to the left or right of it.

    Example:

  • Notes: "A set of text from your notes is here. *team here you want to note something important the employee commented about the team in your 1:1 conversation. And here you end the thought * Then there are some more notes here. And then again you want to note an idea emloyee came up with so you type *idea and note the idea here and end here * and you continue to take notes from your conversation.

    • Extracted information:

  • -*team here you want to note something important the employee commented about the team in your 1:1 conversation. And here you end the thought *
  • -*idea and note the idea here and end here *

    • To try it out, add an employee profile and add some notes for that employee.
